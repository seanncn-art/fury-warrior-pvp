FURY WARRIOR PvP Overview Hero Talents Talents Rotation Stats & Gear Defensive Comps Macros Gallery Fury Warrior Orc — Dual Axes BattlefieldMidnight Season 1 Fury Warrior PvP Guide ⚔️ Arena · 2v2 · 3v3 · Solo Shuffle 📅 Updated April 2026 ✅ Patch 12.0 📋 Overview Fury Warrior is the classic berserker — move in one direction: forward. The spec delivers some of the strongest single-target pressure in arena through relentless aggression, Slaughterhouse healing reduction stacks, and explosive burst windows built around Recklessness and Avatar. Fury has an easy rotation — Rampage is your only spender, everything else generates rage. You'll see results at lower ratings immediately, and there's plenty of room to improve decision-making as you climb. Midnight Changes: Onslaught removed. New Apex talent Rampaging Berserker buffs Rampage and grants a main stat buff per cast. Core rotation unchanged. Slayer remains the stronger arena pick. Your core 3 are almost always locked in — flex the third slot based on matchup. Talent Priority When to Use Slaughterhouse Core Always — your Mortal Wounds effect. 36% healing reduction at full stacks via Rampage. Battlefield Commander Core Always — buffs all shouts, Rallying Cry breaks roots, reduces Intimidating Shout CD. Enduring Rage Core Default 3rd pick — sustains rage and Enrage uptime. Disarm Flex vs Frost DK, Sub Rogue, Arms Warrior, Death Knight — strips weapon-based defensives. Shattering Throw Flex vs Paladin (bubble) or Mage (Ice Block) — dispels magic immunities. Wrecking Throw Flex vs comps with NO magic immunities — raw physical damage. 🔄 Rotation & Playstyle Core rule: All abilities except Rampage generate rage. Dump at 85+ rage with Rampage. Keep Hamstring up at all times. Maintain uptime — lost uptime = lost games. 1 Opener — Force Cooldowns Coordinate kill target with your partner before the gates open. Goal: force enemy defensives early. Charge in → apply Hamstring → Storm Bolt on healer if available → activate Recklessness + Avatar. 2 Maintain Hamstring Always Non-negotiable. Without Hamstring you lose uptime, lose rage, lose games. If your target escapes, hit the nearest enemy until you can reestablish. 3 Rage Priority: BT → Raging Blow → WW → Rampage Bloodthirst → Raging Blow → Whirlwind (for Enrage proc) → dump with Rampage at 85+ rage. Each Rampage applies 4 Slaughterhouse stacks = 12% healing reduction per cast, up to 36% total. 4 Burst Window — Recklessness + Avatar Use together for maximum damage. Fire when the enemy healer is CC'd or your partner has setup. Never burn into Paladin bubble, Ice Block, or Rogue Cloak. 5 Execute Phase (Target below 35%) Execute + Bladestorm = kill window. Go all-in. With Slayer, this is massively amplified. This is when you finish the game. 6 CC Decisions — Offensive vs Defensive Save Intimidating Shout and Storm Bolt for offensive pressure by default. But if your partner is dying, peel — fear the enemy DPS off them. Trading a CC defensively is the correct call when your partner's life is at stake. 📊 Stats & Gear Stat Target Why Strength Max Primary stat — scales all damage Versatility 25%+ Increases damage AND reduces damage taken — essential for PvP survival Mastery High Unshackled Fury — boosts all damage while Enraged (which is always) Haste 15%+ Faster rage generation = more Rampage casts Critical Strike Low Lowest priority — Enrage uptime beats burst crits Slot Choice Notes All Gem Slots Versatile Onyx Best overall for Vers + Mastery priority Unique PvP Gem Enduring Bloodstone Damage reduction while CC'd — one per character Embellishment Elemental Focusing Lens Most consistent throughput — requires 1 gem equipped Cloak Reshii Wraps New in 11.2 — socket with Pure Energizing Fiber 🛡️ Defensive Cooldowns Ability Key Point Usage Enraged Regeneration Usable in stuns Your biggest edge — pop it while stunned to survive burst comps. With Slayer Unhinged, you cannot die for the duration. Defensive Stance Toggle Activate when you're the kill target or expect a setup. Deactivate when you need full damage output. Don't sit in it the whole match. Spell Reflection Proactive Watch the enemy cast bar. Reflecting a big nuke or CC can completely flip a match. Use before impact, not after. Intervene Team save Dash to your partner and redirect melee/ranged onto you. Does NOT block spells. Use frequently when your partner is in danger. Rallying Cry Root break With Battlefield Commander: now breaks all roots on use. Emergency team save when both are under pressure. Intimidating Shout Peel / CC Default: offensive pressure on healer. Situational: peel the enemy DPS off your dying partner. With Battlefield Commander, CD doesn't sync with healer trinket after first use. 👥 Best Compositions Comp Tier Win Condition Warrior + Healer Holy Paladin / Resto Druid / MW Monk S Standard 2v2 backbone. Train kill target relentlessly while healer sustains. Holy Paladin provides bubble + offhealing. Resto Druid offers HoTs and roots for peel. Warrior + Rogue Sub / Outlaw A Rogue sets up Kidney Shot + Blind to create burst windows. High risk — no healer means both must play defensives perfectly. Warrior + WW Monk Windwalker A Coordinated stun chains. Watch for enemy Storm Earth and Fire — save Intimidating Shout to trade with it. Warrior + Mage Frost A Mage Poly + Deep Freeze sets up Warrior burst. Warrior protects Mage with Intervene + Disarm. Bring Shattering Throw for Paladin/Mage mirror matchups. ⌨️ Macros Copy any macro directly into WoW's macro editor (/macro in-game).
